_How To Install iOS Dev Beta's_

    First, Download the profile from the page you just came from (iOS 10 or 11 Download)

    Second, Allow the installation and let your phone reboot

    Third, Plug your phone in and connect it to the internet using WiFi

    Fourth, Go To Settings -> General -> Software Update, after it loads it should show the beta

    Fifth, Hit Download and Install, And allow it to download the update.

    Lastly, Once the download has finished tap install and put in your password. And Voila!

    -Please Note, Times Can Very on Internet and Installation.-
